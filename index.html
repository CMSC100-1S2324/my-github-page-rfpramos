Projects

BooKonnect

BooKonnect is a book donation platform that allows book donors to choose which books to donate to Children Readers of Africa. Through "swiping right" and "swiping left", a donor get to choose which books he wants to donate.

WebiNow

It is a platform that allows you to find webinars that are soon to happen, so that you'll be able to have the opportunity to learn through participating them. You can view these webinars through catergories so that you'll be able to find what you are looking for.

ContraCovid-2019

ContraCovid is an interactive simulation of the spread of the SARSCov called Coronavirus 2019. It aims to simulate an SEIR Mathematical model through visual representation of animationed marbles.
